quietNotify for Hairdressers

Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 5, 2026  ·  Last updated: May 5, 2026

1. Who We Are

quietNotify ("we," "us," or "our") operates the quietNotify for Hairdressers platform — an appointment management and client notification service for solo stylists and small salons, available at getquietnotify.com. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our platform.

If you have questions, contact us at support@getquietnotify.com.

2. Information We Collect

Account holders (stylists & salon owners)

  • Name and email address (registration)
  • Business name and contact information
  • Billing information processed by Stripe (we never store raw card numbers)
  • Service menu, availability schedule, and appointment settings
  • IP address and browser type (for security and fraud prevention)

Client data entered by stylists

As a stylist or salon owner, you enter data about your clients. This may include:

  • Client name, email address, and phone number
  • Appointment dates, times, and selected services
  • Appointment notes and payment history
  • No-show or cancellation records

You are the data controller for your clients' personal information. You are responsible for having a lawful basis to collect and process that information and for informing your clients that you use quietNotify to manage their appointments and send them reminders.

Client-submitted data (public booking page)

If you use our public booking page feature, your clients may submit:

  • Name, email address, and phone number
  • Preferred service and appointment time

By submitting a booking, clients agree that their contact information will be used to confirm their appointment and send them reminders.

Usage data

  • Pages visited and features used within the platform
  • Session timestamps and error logs

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To deliver the service — manage your appointment calendar, client records, and stylist dashboard.
  • To send appointment reminders — automated SMS and email reminders are sent to clients using the contact details you or they provide.
  • To process payments — your subscription and any client deposit or payment links are handled through Stripe.
  • To confirm bookings — confirmation messages are sent via email and/or SMS to clients who book through your public booking page.
  • To improve the platform — aggregate, de-identified usage data helps us identify bugs and build better features.
  • To communicate with you — product updates, billing receipts, and support responses.

We do not sell your data or your clients' data to any third party for marketing purposes.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following sub-processors to operate the platform:

Provider Purpose Data shared
Stripe Payment processing Billing email, payment amounts
Twilio SMS delivery Client phone number, message content
SMTP provider Email delivery Recipient email, message content
Railway Cloud hosting & database All platform data (encrypted at rest)

Each provider operates under its own privacy policy and data processing agreements.

5. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. If you cancel your subscription, we retain your data for 90 days to allow reactivation, after which it is permanently deleted. Client records are deleted when you remove them from the platform or when your account is closed.

Stripe retains billing records independently per their data retention policy.

6. Security

We use industry-standard safeguards including encrypted database connections, HTTPS-only access, hashed passwords, and CSRF protection on all forms. We do not store credit card numbers — all payment data is tokenized and held by Stripe.

No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. In the event of a data breach that affects your personal information, we will notify you as required by applicable law.

7. Cookies

We use a single session cookie to keep you logged in. Your clients' public booking page does not set any tracking cookies. We do not use third-party advertising cookies or tracking pixels.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Object to or restrict certain processing
  • Data portability (receive your data in a machine-readable format)

Clients who wish to request deletion of their data should contact the stylist or salon directly, as the stylist controls that data. To exercise rights against quietNotify as a company, email support@getquietnotify.com. We will respond within 30 days.

9. Children's Privacy

Our platform is intended for adults operating hair care businesses. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under the age of 18. If a client is a minor, we require the stylist to have obtained appropriate parental consent before entering the client's information into the platform.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated by email to active account holders at least 14 days before they take effect. Continued use of the platform after that date constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
