How we build

The quietNotify standard

We build differently than most SaaS companies, intentionally and permanently.

Narrow by design

One product, one job. We do not add features to justify pricing. We charge fairly for a problem we solve fully.

Ships fast, stays small

We ship a working product quickly, then improve. No years-long platform plays. If it is not useful in week one, we cut it.

Self-serve or nothing

If you need a sales call to understand the product, we have failed. Setup takes minutes. Pricing is public. No demos required.

Honest pricing

Flat rates, public plans. No enterprise pricing games, no per-seat tricks for solo operators. You know exactly what you pay.

Boring UI on purpose

No charts for their own sake. No dark patterns. Calm, plain interfaces that let you see what matters and get out of the way.

Opinionated defaults

We pick the right defaults so you do not have to configure everything. Most users should never touch a setting.
